# Medias


## Média Aritmética

$$
	\overset{-}x = \frac{1}{2} \sum_{i=1}^\limits{n} = \frac{x1 + x2 + x3 + ...}{n}
$$


## Média Ponderada
$$
\overset{-}x = \frac{x1*n1 + x2*n2 + x3*n3 + ... + nk*nk}{n1 + n2 + n3 + ... + nk} = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^\limits{k} n_{i} * k_{j}
$$


## Mediana
- Para Casos Impares

$$
	MD = x(\frac{n+1}{2}) 
$$

- Para Casos Pares

$$
	MD = \frac{x{\frac{n}{2}} + x(\frac{n}{2}+1)}{2} 
$$
